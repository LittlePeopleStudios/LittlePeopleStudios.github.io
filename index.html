About Us

Little People Studios is a passionate team of game developers dedicated to creating unique and memorable gaming experiences. Founded in 2023, we specialize in indie games that combine innovative mechanics with compelling storytelling.

Our team comes from diverse backgrounds but shares a common love for games that challenge conventions and spark imagination. We believe in the power of games to tell stories, bring people together, and create moments of wonder.
